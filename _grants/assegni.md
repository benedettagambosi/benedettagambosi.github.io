## Network-Based Analysis of Multimodal Brain Data in Physiological and Pathological Conditions  

**Funding:** PRIN 2020 – *ACT2: Acting Together: How Motor Styles Shape Action Prediction and Brain-to-Brain Connectivity in Typical and Autistic Populations*  
**Grant ID:** 20207S3NB8  
**Institution:** Sapienza University of Rome  
**Duration:** 01/10/2024 – 30/09/2026  

This project focuses on network-based analysis of multimodal brain data to investigate physiological and pathological conditions, with particular attention to motor interaction and autism spectrum conditions.

## Explainable Neural Networks and Trustworthy AI in Biomedicine and Neuroscience  

**Funding:** Horizon Europe – I3LUNG  
**Grant Agreement:** No. 101057695  
**Institution:** Politecnico di Milano  
**Duration:** 01/10/2023 – 30/09/2024  

The project addresses the development and application of explainable and trustworthy AI methods in biomedicine and neuroscience, with a focus on interpretability and clinical relevance.

## Bioinspired Neural Networks for Multiarea Brain Simulations  

**Funding:** H2020 – Human Brain Project SGA3  
**Grant Agreement:** No. 945539  
**Institution:** Politecnico di Milano  
**Duration:** 01/10/2021 – 30/09/2023  

This project focused on bioinspired neural network models for large-scale and multiarea brain simulations, contributing to computational approaches for studying brain dynamics across scales.
